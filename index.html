Nothing You Don't.

Real-Time Slot Tracking

Live updates on available, occupied, and reserved slots across all campus zones using sensor integration.

📱

Instant Booking

Book a slot from your phone or laptop in seconds. Choose your zone, time, and vehicle type seamlessly.

🔐

QR Code Entry

A unique QR code is generated per booking. Scan at the gate for frictionless, contactless entry and exit.

Smart Notifications

Get alerts when your booking is confirmed, when you're 10 minutes away from expiry, and more.

Stress-Free Parking.
